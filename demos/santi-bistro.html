Notre Histoire

Where every dish
tells a story

Santi Bistro was born from a love of honest cooking. Our menu changes with the seasons, guided by what's freshest from our network of local farms and purveyors in the Hudson Valley and Long Island.

We believe the best meal is one where you forget to look at your phone. Our dining room is unhurried, warm, and filled with the kind of conversation that only a great table can inspire.

"We cook with the seasons because that's when food tastes most like itself."

— Chef Santiago Moreau, Founder
