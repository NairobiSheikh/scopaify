Our Story

Where fire meets
precision

Ember Grill was born from a singular obsession: the perfect steak. Every cut is hand-selected from small farms, dry-aged in-house for a minimum of 28 days, and finished over live fire at 600 degrees. We don't follow trends. We follow the craft.

Our dining room is designed for those who appreciate the ritual of a great meal — the anticipation, the aroma, the first cut. Whether you're celebrating or simply making a Tuesday extraordinary, Ember Grill delivers an experience worth returning for.
